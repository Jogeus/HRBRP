About High Rock Border Roleplay

High Rock Border Roleplay (HRBRP) is one of the largest interactive roleplay communities for the highly popular Roblox game Emergency Response: Liberty County (ER:LC). Its highly realistic simulation of police and border security scenarios has made HRBRP one of the largest and most active border roleplay servers for the ER:LC community, boasting an incredible Discord membership of over 8,500 members.

At the core of HRBRP is the focus on intense high-pressure, role-play-intensive activity on the management and control of an artificial border, an aspect that appeals to those who enjoy the challenge and sophistication of police and security work. Individuals play roles of border officers, police officers, and civilians as they patrol the border and perform sophisticated customs and immigration screening, thereby creating an intense range of experience that can switch from heart-pounding to nail-biting within an instant.
